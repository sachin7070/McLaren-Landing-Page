Maclaren 720S

McLaren's 720S, a pinnacle of automotive engineering, boasts a 91% innovation rate compared to its forerunner. Housing McLaren's potent M840T engine, it delivers 720 PS (530 kW; 710 hp) at 7,500 rpm, paired with a lighter ProActive Chassis Control II suspension system. Its MonoCage II chassis offers enhanced rigidity, while the interior blends luxury with performance, featuring digital displays and race-inspired elements. Achieving 0-100 km/h in 2.9 seconds, it excels in both power and efficiency, setting a new benchmark in supercar design.
